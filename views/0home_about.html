About the Student Project Management System

Our platform is designed to streamline project management for students, faculty, and staff at IIITP. It simplifies group formation, project tracking, faculty allocation, and more to ensure seamless collaboration and communication between all parties involved.

Our Mission

To provide a centralized and easy-to-use system that enhances the academic project experience for both students and faculty by making project management efficient, organized, and transparent.

Key Features

Student Management

Students can easily register, create project groups, and choose faculty advisors with a clear, structured workflow.

Faculty Supervision

Faculty members can efficiently manage, monitor, and provide feedback on student projects through an organized dashboard.

Admin Control

Admins oversee the entire process, from student registrations to faculty assignments, ensuring smooth operations.
